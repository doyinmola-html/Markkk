📅 May 2025  ·  Cohort News
Cohort 2 Is Now Open — Applications Live for All 5 Skill Tracks
After a successful first cohort that saw students master graphics design from the ground up, Marketonomy is proud to open applications for Cohort 2. This time, all five skill tracks — Graphics Design, Digital Marketing, Front-End Development, AI & Machine Learning, and Trading — are available for enrolment. Cohort 2 features restructured modules, more mentor touchpoints, and a dedicated peer community channel.
Spots are limited. Students who enrol early will have priority access to mentor selection and the new project-based curriculum launching this cohort.
